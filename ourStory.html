Our Story

OUR PASSION AND PURPOSE IS BRINGING TREND-FACING, MODERN HAIR TO DALLAS

Spectral Salon

After living in various cities across the country, our founders moved back to their home town of Dallas, Texas in 2017. They were stunned by the lack of modern salon offerings in their community- so they did something about it. Our founders opened the doors of the first Spectral Salon to fulfill an unmet desire for high quality, contemporary hair care in Texas.

Trained and apprenticed in Los Angeles, Chicago, and New York City, our founders envisioned Spectral Salon to be a marriage of urban style and Texas roots.
